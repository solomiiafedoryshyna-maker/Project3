About HealthSpecialist

Our mission is to combine modern medicine, human care, and convenient access to healthcare — so you always feel healthy and confident. We provide timely, evidence-based care with a personalized approach. From family medicine to cardiology, our team focuses on prevention, early diagnosis, and a smooth, comfortable recovery for every patient. We believe that true health comes from trust, compassion, and continuous support at every stage of your life.

Our story

  1. 2016 — The Idea

    We started as a small practice focused on rapid primary care and prevention.

  2. 2019 — Expansion

    Opened diagnostic and rehabilitation units to support full recovery cycles.

  3. 2023 — Digital first

    Launched online booking, patient portal, and same-day telemedicine.

  4. Today — Multispecialty

Frequently asked questions

How do I book an appointment?

Use online booking in your account or call our front desk. Same-day slots appear at 08:00.

Do you offer telemedicine?

Yes. We provide secure video consultations for follow-ups and non-urgent visits.

How will I get my test results?

Your results are published in your secure online account and sent as notifications.
